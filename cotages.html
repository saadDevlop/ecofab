COTAGES

Our custom cotages frames consist of high-quality steel elements that provide exceptional durability, weather resistance, and structural integrity. EcoFab utilizes advanced steel framing techniques to create beautiful and long-lasting cotages that perfectly blend aesthetics with functionality.

Each cotage is meticulously designed and crafted to meet your specific requirements, incorporating modern architectural elements with traditional charm. Our steel framing solutions ensure superior strength, flexibility in design, and rapid construction timelines.

WHY STEEL FRAMING FOR COTAGES?

  • Durability - Steel framing provides exceptional strength and longevity, ensuring your cotage stands strong against various weather conditions and the test of time.
  • Design Flexibility - Our steel framing systems allow for creative architectural designs, open floor plans, and custom features that make your cotage unique.
  • Energy Efficiency - Modern steel construction techniques combined with proper insulation create energy-efficient structures that help reduce utility costs.
