EXHIBITION BOOTHS

Exhibition booth frames consist of structural elements that provide weather-tight, acoustic and fire resistance properties. Dynamic Steel Frame utilizes light gauge steel (LGS) framing for its advanced performance and customization properties. Steel is the ultimate option for exhibition facades thanks to its ability to support a wide range of industrial and commercial cladding panels.

Our exhibition booth frames have a unique ability to be clad in varying materials including insulated metal panels, large boards, metallic panels and even terracotta tiles. The result is an endless array of façade treatments, shapes and structures that can be tailored to your creative vision.

WHY STEEL FRAMING FOR EXHIBITION BOOTHS?

  • Safety - The strength of steel combined with its noncombustible qualities achieves a high resistance to natural disasters including earthquakes and hurricanes. As the cornerstone of your exhibition booth, steel provides peace of mind when it comes to protecting your structure.
  • Versatility - Due to its strength, Light Gauge Steel can span longer lengths, allowing for larger panels and increased design flexibility.
  • Performance - Our TRUECORE® steel is engineered to ensure stiff, rust and rot resistance, providing low maintenance longevity against the elements. Even termites don't stand a chance.
